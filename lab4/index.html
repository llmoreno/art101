For our initial task, we decided to explain how to paint on a canvas, step-by-step.

How to Paint a Canvas

  1. Prime the canvas.
  2. Gather materials. You'll need a canvas, paintbrushes of various sizes, a cup of water, paints, a palette, a pencil, and a papertowel or rag to dry brushes on.
  3. Sketch your idea onto the canvas using the pencil. Be sure to draw lightly.
  4. Once your sketch is complete, you can start painting. Paint the background layer first.
  5. After your background layer is dry you can start on your next layer. Keep adding layers until your painting looks how you want it to.
  6. Optional: Seal your painting with a fixative.
